About Us

Welcome to Bharat Plywoods, a trusted name in premium plywood and interior solutions since 1992. Conveniently located at Sapna Sangeeta Road, Indore, we are a family-owned business led by Mr. Govind Agarwal and Mr. Yash Agarwal, dedicated to serving customers with quality products and reliable service.


For over three decades, we have been supplying a comprehensive range of plywood, laminates, natural veneers, MDF boards, acrylic sheets, louvers, adhesives, and other engineered wood products. Whether you are designing a home, furnishing an office, or executing a large-scale commercial project, we provide solutions that combine durability, functionality, and aesthetics.


Our commitment goes beyond products. We believe in offering honest guidance, personalized attention, and dependable support to every customer. From homeowners and interior designers to architects, contractors, and builders, clients across Indore trust us for quality materials and lasting relationships.
